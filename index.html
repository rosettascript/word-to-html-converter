About This Word to HTML Converter

This word to HTML converter is a free, client-side tool designed to clean HTML output from Microsoft Word documents. When you paste content from Word into a CMS like Shopify or any other platform, it often includes excessive inline styles, classes, and formatting bloat that can break your site's design and slow page load times.

Our word to HTML converter strips away those unwanted styles while preserving the semantic structure of your content—headings, paragraphs, lists, links, and emphasis remain intact. Choose from three specialized output modes (Regular, Shopify Blogs, Shopify Shoppables) tailored for different use cases, and enjoy instant processing with a full-width workspace. Your data never leaves your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security with this word to HTML converter.

Built with vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies, no tracking. 100% free word to HTML converter.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use this word to HTML converter?

Using this word to HTML converter is simple: paste your Word-generated HTML into the input area on the left, select your desired output mode from the dropdown (Regular, Shopify Blogs, or Shopify Shoppables), and the cleaned HTML appears instantly in the output area. You can customize the conversion with optional features like adding strong tags to headers or converting internal links to relative paths. Once processed, copy the cleaned HTML to your clipboard or download it as a file.

What's the difference between the three output modes?

Regular: This word to HTML converter mode provides basic cleaning—removes inline styles, classes, and unnecessary attributes while preserving semantic structure like headings, paragraphs, and lists.

Shopify Blogs: The word to HTML converter optimizes content for Shopify blog posts with special formatting for "Key Takeaways" sections, list combining, external link attributes (target="_blank"), and optional paragraph spacers between sections.

Shopify Shoppables: This word to HTML converter mode creates compact formatting for Shopify product descriptions with aggressive whitespace minification and streamlined HTML output.

Is my content safe? Where is it stored?

Yes, your content is completely safe with this word to HTML converter. All processing happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Nothing is sent to a server, and nothing is stored anywhere—not even locally. Your content never leaves your device, ensuring 100% privacy and security. This word to HTML converter runs completely offline once the page is loaded.

What happens to images in my HTML?

This word to HTML converter automatically removes all images from the cleaned output to avoid broken image links and bloated base64-encoded image data that Microsoft Word often includes. The input area displays your original HTML with images for reference, but the final converted HTML excludes them. This keeps your output clean, lightweight, and ready for your CMS where you'll typically upload images separately.

Can I use this word to HTML converter for large documents?

Absolutely! This word to HTML converter can handle documents of all sizes with instant processing as you paste. The full-width layout provides ample space for working with lengthy blog posts, articles, or product descriptions. The converter processes everything client-side in your browser, so there are no file size restrictions or upload limits. Whether you're converting a short paragraph or a 10,000-word article, this word to HTML converter delivers instant results.

What browsers work with this word to HTML converter?

This word to HTML converter works in all modern browsers (last 2 versions): Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge. It requires ES6 module support and modern JavaScript APIs, which are standard in all current browsers. The word to HTML converter is fully responsive and works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, though a larger screen provides the best experience for working with extensive HTML code.

What optional features does this word to HTML converter offer?

This word to HTML converter includes several customization options: Put <strong> tags in headers (default ON in Shopify modes) wraps header content in strong tags for added emphasis; Convert internal links to relative paths automatically detects your domain and converts absolute URLs to relative paths; and Remove paragraph spacers (Shopify Blogs only) lets you control whether to keep or remove spacing paragraphs between sections. These options give you precise control over your HTML output.
